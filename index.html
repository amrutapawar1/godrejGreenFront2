Overview

A Snapshot of Our Real Estate Expertise

Godrej Greenfront is the latest and most exquisite residential offering within Godrej Park World Hinjewadi, seamlessly blending urban sophistication with lush green serenity. Strategically located in Godrej Phase 1, it ensures effortless connectivity to Pune’s prime destinations while providing a tranquil escape from the city's bustle.Following the success of Godrej Gale Hinjewadi, a previous phase within the township, Godrej Green Front Hinjewadi takes luxury living to the next level. As the current launch, The Greenfront at Godrej Park World features expansive green spaces, a grand clubhouse, and vibrant pedestrian zones, offering a serene yet well-connected lifestyle.
